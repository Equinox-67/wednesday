Quadri's Airline

Quadri's Airline is the largest airline and one of the two flag carriers of the African airline industry. Based in Lagos, Nigeria the airline is a subsidiary of the The dangote foundation, which is owned by Aliko Dangote. It is also the largest airline in Africa, operating over 3,600 flights per week from its hub at terminal 3 of the Nigeria international Airport before the covid-19 pandemic. It operates to more than 150 cities in 80 countries across 6 continents through its fleet of nearly 300 aircraft. Cargo activities are undertaken by Quadri's skyCargo.
